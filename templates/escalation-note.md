# Template: Escalation Note to General Counsel

## 1. Purpose
This template structures formal escalations to the General Counsel when automated rules identify high-risk triggers or policy conflicts.

## 2. When to Use
Use this template when a contract, DPA, vendor, or product launch triggers an `escalate` risk classification, or when standard contract playbook rules cannot resolve a negotiation conflict.

## 3. Intake Questions (To be completed by Triage Owner)
*   **Escalated By**: [Name / Role, e.g. Triage Analyst]
*   **Date of Triage**: [YYYY-MM-DD]
*   **Matter Name / Reference**: [e.g. Acme Health MSA Triage]
*   **Trigger Reason**: [Identify specific deterministic rule triggered, e.g. regulated customer sector, custom audit rights]

## 4. Review Questions (To be completed by Legal / Risk Reviewer)
*   **Background Context**: [Summary of the matter and transaction status]
*   **Identified Risks**: [Details of the legal, privacy, or security concerns]
*   **Business Position**: [Why the team is requesting the change/accepting the risk]
*   **Proposed Compromise**: [Draft option to resolve the issue]

## 5. Output
*   **GC Decision**: [Approve / Approve with Conditions / Reject]
*   **Remediation Plan**: [Steps to implement the GC decision]
*   **Playbook Exception Logged**: [Yes / No]

## 6. Escalation Triggers
This note will be automatically sent to the General Counsel's priority inbox. No further triggers required.

## 7. Playbook Update
*   *Once the GC logs a decision, update the Contract Playbook to include the decision as a precedent for future identical requests.*
