# Policy: Human-in-the-Loop & Legal Advice Disclaimer

## 1. Objective
This policy establishes the rules for human review and validation of all outputs generated by automated legal triage systems, templates, and scripts.

## 2. No Automated Legal Advice
> [!IMPORTANT]
> **This repository, its templates, schemas, and scoring scripts DO NOT produce legal advice.** All automated classifications, risk scores, and check-lists are for triage and operational efficiency only.

No workflow inside this system produces final, binding legal advice automatically. Consequential legal decisions, contract approvals, regulatory filings, and vendor approvals require review and sign-off by a qualified lawyer or an accountable internal department head.

## 3. Human Review Requirements
A qualified human reviewer (Product Counsel, General Counsel, DPO, or CISO) must inspect and validate:
*   Any matter classified as `high` or `escalate` by the risk-scoring engine.
*   Any final customer-facing DPA modifications or MSA markups.
*   The output of any AI vendor governance review prior to vendor signup.
*   Pre-launch product intakes affecting regulated customer groups.

## 4. Enforcement
Decisions made solely based on automated output without human verification constitute a violation of corporate compliance policies.
